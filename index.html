MARTIN LINDBERG

I am an engineer in Media Technology from Stockholm, Sweden. My educational background is from KTH - Royal Institute of Technology with a M.Sc in Interactive Media Technology.

My passion is designing fantastic experiences with a combination of UX, business and a marketing mindset.

I am currently employed as a teacher’s assistant in Python programming at KTH and have a lot of experience in teaching and communication. On the side I work as event staff for One Motion IMC at different fairs and exhibitions.

Apart from work my interest include gaming, producing a podcast, acting and tabletop RPGs.
